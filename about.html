About Velox

We're passionate about transforming waste into wonder. Since 2022, Velox has been pioneering sustainable recycling solutions that turn plastic bottles into high-quality polyester products.

Our Mission

To accelerate the transition to a circular economy by transforming plastic waste into premium sustainable products, creating value for businesses while protecting our planet for future generations.

Our Vision

A world where waste becomes a resource, where every plastic bottle finds new life as a sustainable product, and where circular manufacturing is the global standard.

Our Story

The Beginning (2021)

Dr. Maria Rodriguez and James Chen met at a sustainability conference, sharing a vision to solve the plastic waste crisis through innovative recycling technology. Their combined expertise in materials science and engineering laid the foundation for Velox.

Foundation (2022)

Velox was officially founded with the mission to create high-quality recycled polyester products. Our first facility began operations processing 500,000 bottles per month, focusing on fiberfill for the bedding industry.

Growth (2023)

Rapid expansion saw us double our processing capacity and achieve our first major certifications. We formed strategic partnerships with leading bedding manufacturers and expanded our product line to include polyester thread.

Innovation (2024)

Investment in advanced sorting and processing technology increased our efficiency by 40%. We launched our finished pillow line and expanded into commercial markets, serving over 200 B2B customers.

Today (2025)

Velox processes nearly 3 million bottles annually, employs 147 people, and continues to innovate in sustainable manufacturing. We're expanding into new markets while maintaining our commitment to quality and environmental impact.
