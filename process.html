Our Recycling Process

Transforming waste into wonder through our innovative six-step process that converts plastic bottles into premium polyester thread and sustainable products.

01

Collection & Sorting

We source sorted PET bottles from certified partners and municipal recycling programs. Each bottle is inspected to ensure it meets our quality standards for color, contamination levels, and material composition.

  • Partnership with certified waste management facilities
  • Automated sorting technology for purity
  • Quality inspection at every stage
02

Cleaning & Preparation

Bottles undergo thorough washing to remove labels, adhesives, and any contaminants. The clean bottles are then shredded into small flakes, creating the raw material for the next stage of processing.

  • Hot wash and caustic treatment systems
  • Label and cap removal technology
  • Precision shredding to optimal flake size
03

Pelletizing

Purified flakes are melted and reformed into uniform PET pellets. This critical step ensures consistent material properties and removes any remaining impurities through advanced filtration systems.

  • Controlled temperature melting process
  • Multi-stage filtration for purity
  • Consistent pellet size and quality
04

Extrusion

PET pellets are heated and extruded through precision spinnerets to create continuous filament fibers. This process transforms solid plastic into workable thread material with specific diameter and strength characteristics.

  • Precision spinneret technology
  • Controlled fiber diameter and properties
  • Continuous production monitoring
05

Texturizing & Drawing

Fibers are drawn, crimped, and textured to achieve the desired properties for different applications. This process enhances durability, elasticity, and feel of the final thread.

  • Variable draw ratio for different applications
  • Crimping for enhanced loft and feel
  • Quality testing at multiple points
06

Final Products

The finished polyester thread and fiberfill are packaged and prepared for shipment to manufacturers who create pillows, cushions, and other soft goods, completing the circular economy cycle.

  • Multiple product formats available
  • Custom packaging and labeling
  • Full traceability and certification
