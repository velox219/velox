Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our recycling process, products, and sustainability practices.

Process & Technology

We accept clear and colored PET bottles (recycling code #1), including water bottles, soda bottles, and food containers. We work with certified collection partners to ensure proper sorting and only process bottles that meet our quality standards for contamination levels and material composition.

Our multi-stage cleaning process includes hot wash treatment, caustic cleaning, and advanced filtration systems. Each batch undergoes rigorous testing for contaminants, and we maintain full traceability throughout the process. All our products meet FDA requirements for food-grade applications.

Bottles that don't meet our quality standards are sorted and sent to appropriate recycling partners for other applications. We maintain a zero-waste policy in our operations, ensuring that no plastic waste goes to landfills from our facility.

Our process uses 60% less energy compared to virgin polyester production. We've invested in energy-efficient machinery, heat recovery systems, and renewable energy sources to minimize our carbon footprint while maintaining high production standards.

Products & Applications

Our recycled polyester maintains the same quality, strength, and performance characteristics as virgin polyester while using 60% less energy and producing 70% fewer CO2 emissions during production. The only difference is the positive environmental impact.

Our recycled polyester thread and fiberfill are perfect for pillows, cushions, comforters, sleeping bags, jackets, and upholstery. The material is also suitable for automotive applications, filtration systems, and technical textiles requiring durability and performance.

Yes, we can customize thread specifications including denier (ranging from 1.2 to 15), cut length (2-64mm), and various treatments like silicone finishing or anti-microbial properties. Minimum order quantities apply for custom specifications.

Yes, our recycled polyester products are naturally hypoallergenic, resist dust mites, and are machine washable. They're OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified, ensuring they're free from harmful substances and safe for direct skin contact.

Quality & Certifications

Our products are certified under Global Recycled Standard (GRS), OEKO-TEX Standard 100, and ISO 9001:2015. These certifications verify our recycled content, safety standards, and quality management systems. We also maintain FDA compliance for food-contact applications.

We employ rigorous quality control at every stage, including incoming material inspection, in-process monitoring, and final product testing. Our ISO 9001 certified quality management system ensures consistency, and we maintain detailed batch records for full traceability.

We conduct comprehensive testing including tensile strength, elongation, thermal properties, color fastness, and contamination analysis. Each batch is tested for heavy metals, volatile compounds, and other harmful substances according to international standards.

Shipping & Logistics

Standard products ship within 2-3 weeks of order confirmation. Custom specifications may require 4-6 weeks depending on complexity. During peak seasons (Q4), lead times may be extended. We recommend placing orders well in advance for critical deadlines.

Fiberfill is compressed and packaged in polyethylene bags or bales for efficient shipping. Thread is wound on spools or cones and packaged in protective containers. We use recyclable packaging materials whenever possible and optimize packaging for minimal environmental impact.
