From Waste to Wonder

Our six-step process transforms discarded plastic bottles into premium polyester thread and sustainable products.

Collection

We source sorted PET bottles from certified partners.

Cleaning

Bottles are washed, labels removed, and flakes created.

Pelletizing

Purified flakes are melted into uniform PET pellets.

Extrusion

Pellets are extruded into continuous filament fiber.

Texturizing

Fibers are drawn, crimped, and spun into durable thread.

Products

Thread and fiberfill become pillows and more.
